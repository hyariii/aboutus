You Have Questions, We Have Answers

Krishipath is designed for all farmers looking to improve their agricultural practices. Whether you're a small-scale farmer with limited resources, a medium-sized farm looking to optimize yields, or an agricultural cooperative seeking data-driven solutions, our platform adapts to your specific needs and local conditions.
We offer comprehensive agricultural tools including our AI-powered farming assistant chatbot, personalized soil health analysis, weather forecasting, fertilizer calculators, and crop recommendation systems. Our platform combines local agricultural knowledge with smart technology to provide actionable farming insights.
Our crop management tools analyze your soil data, local weather patterns, and crop characteristics to provide personalized planting schedules, irrigation recommendations, and pest management advice. We combine traditional farming wisdom with data science to help you maximize yields while minimizing resource use.
We're developing offline functionality and SMS-based services for areas with poor connectivity. Currently, basic features are available via low-bandwidth mobile web, and we're partnering with local agricultural centers to provide access points in remote communities.
Our recommendations are based on verified agricultural research and localized data. While we maintain 85-90% accuracy for most common crops, we always recommend combining our suggestions with your practical farming experience and consulting local agricultural experts when making major decisions.
